NovaScan — Request Data Deletion

Last updated: 15 July 2026 · NovaScan by Pawtap Studios

This page explains how to delete data associated with your use of NovaScan, the document-scanning app by Pawtap Studios (com.pawtapstudios.novascan).

Scanned documents and photos

NovaScan stores your scanned documents and pages only on your device — nothing is uploaded to any server we operate. You can delete this data yourself, immediately, in two ways:

  • Delete an individual document — open NovaScan, find the document in your library, and choose Delete. It's removed from your device right away.
  • Delete everything at once — uninstall NovaScan from your device. This removes the app's entire local database, including every scanned document and page.

No request to us is needed for either of these — you're always in full control of this data.

Purchase and subscription records

If you've purchased NovaScan Premium (subscription or lifetime), your purchase is processed by Google Play Billing and managed through RevenueCat, a subscription-management service. NovaScan itself does not store your payment details. Records of the transaction (such as the product purchased and purchase date) are retained by Google Play and RevenueCat according to their own data retention policies:

  • To manage or view your purchase history directly, see your Google Play subscriptions page.
  • To request deletion of the purchase record RevenueCat holds on our behalf, email us at pawtapstudios@gmail.com with the subject "Data deletion request" and we'll forward the request to RevenueCat and confirm once it's actioned.

Advertising identifier

If you use NovaScan's free, ad-supported tier, Google AdMob collects your device's advertising identifier while ads are shown. This is tied to your device, not an account we hold — you can reset or opt out of ad personalization at any time in your device's own Android settings (Settings → Privacy → Ads), or remove it entirely by purchasing NovaScan Premium, which stops all ad requests.

Anything else

For any other question about data NovaScan or its service providers hold, email pawtapstudios@gmail.com and we'll respond directly.
