NovaScan Privacy Policy

Last updated: 15 July 2026

NovaScan ("the app") is developed by Pawtap Studios. This policy explains what data the app collects, why, and how it's handled. NovaScan does not require an account or login to use.

Camera and documents

NovaScan uses your device's camera, with your permission, to capture photos of documents you choose to scan. Requesting camera access happens only when you tap Scan — never automatically on app start. Scanned pages and the documents you create are stored only on your device, in the app's local database. NovaScan does not upload your scans or documents to any server, and no one other than you can access them through the app. Deleting a document in the app, or uninstalling NovaScan, removes this data from your device.

Advertising

NovaScan shows ads to free-tier users, served through Google AdMob. AdMob collects your device's advertising identifier and related technical information to select and measure ads, and may use this data consistent with Google's advertising policies. Where required (for example, in the EEA/UK), NovaScan asks for your consent before requesting personalized ads, through Google's User Messaging Platform. Purchasing NovaScan Premium removes ads entirely.

Purchases and subscriptions

NovaScan's subscriptions and one-time purchase are processed through Google Play Billing and managed with RevenueCat, a subscription-management service. RevenueCat receives purchase and subscription data (such as product identifiers, purchase dates, and a device/account identifier needed to validate and restore your purchase) in order to determine whether your account has active Premium access. See RevenueCat's privacy policy for details on how they handle this data.

What NovaScan does not collect

NovaScan does not require you to create an account, and does not collect your name, email address, or other personal identifiers directly. NovaScan does not sell your data.

Children's privacy

NovaScan is a general-purpose productivity tool and is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, the "Last updated" date above will change accordingly. Continued use of NovaScan after a change means you accept the updated policy.

Contact
